about us

A love story with some bumps and many travels.

We met in 2015...

when I started working at a company where he was already employed. Initially, we didn't get along at all. There was no "match," no connection, just mutual annoyance. We didn't talk or interact, only the minimum required for work.

This went on for a few years until 2017 when we were "forced" to work together on a project. We started talking, talking, and talking, and we became friends. We talked about our relationships, work stuff, dreams, and aspirations.

In 2018...

he started traveling a lot for work, so we saw each other less and only talked when he was at the office. I started feeling a strange sense of missing him. We exchanged a few messages, nothing significant. At that time, we were both single.

He got promoted, and we decided to go out for lunch to celebrate. On the way back from lunch,
